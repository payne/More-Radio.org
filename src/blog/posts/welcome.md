Welcome to the official More Radio blog! We're excited to launch this new platform to share news, technical articles, operating tips, and stories from our amateur radio community.

## What to Expect

Here on the blog, you'll find:

- **Club news and announcements** - Stay up to date with club activities
- **Technical articles** - Deep dives into equipment, antennas, and operating techniques
- **Member spotlights** - Get to know your fellow operators
- **Contest reports** - Recaps and tips from our contest activities
- **Project showcases** - See what members are building

## Get Involved

We welcome contributions from all club members! If you have an article idea, project to share, or story to tell, please reach out to the editorial team.

Topics we'd love to hear about:

- Your station setup
- Interesting contacts you've made
- Technical projects you've completed
- Tips for new operators
- Contest strategies and experiences
- Vintage equipment restoration

## Stay Connected

Be sure to check back regularly for new posts. You can also:

- Attend our monthly club meetings
- Check into our weekly nets
- Follow announcements on the repeater

We look forward to sharing this journey with you. 73!

*- The More Radio Team*
