## Latest News

Stay up to date with the latest happenings in our amateur radio community.

---

### Field Day Planning Underway

We're excited to announce that planning for this year's ARRL Field Day is in full swing! This is amateur radio's largest on-air event and a great opportunity for public outreach.

If you'd like to help with planning or participate, please join us at the next club meeting.

---

### New Repeater Online

Our new 70cm repeater is now operational! Thanks to everyone who helped with the installation. The repeater offers excellent coverage of the local area.

**Frequency:** 444.000 MHz (+5 MHz offset)
**PL Tone:** 100.0 Hz

---

### Licensing Class Starting Soon

We'll be offering a Technician licensing class for anyone interested in getting their amateur radio license. Classes will cover all the material needed to pass the FCC exam.

More details coming soon. Spread the word to anyone who might be interested in joining our hobby!

---

### Club Meeting Recap

Last month's meeting featured a presentation on antenna modeling software. Thanks to all who attended! Slides from the presentation are available upon request.
