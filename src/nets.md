## Area Amateur Radio Nets

### 9 PM Friday Technical Ragchew NET

**Frequency:** 145.235 MHz (W0JJK repeater) — offset: –, CTCSS: 131.8 Hz  
**Schedule:** Fridays at 9:00 PM Central Time  
**Location:** Omaha, Nebraska

A weekly round-robin ragchew and technical discussion NET. Open to all licensed operators (Technician and above) who can access the repeater. Topics include shack updates, antenna projects, and general ham radio conversation.

Tune to 145.235 MHz, set your CTCSS tone to 131.8 Hz, and check in when net control solicits stations.

More info: [235.moreradio.club](https://235.moreradio.club/)
