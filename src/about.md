## Who We Are

More Radio is a community of amateur radio operators dedicated to advancing the art and science of ham radio. We welcome operators of all experience levels, from newly licensed hams to seasoned veterans with decades on the air.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to:

- **Promote amateur radio** in our local community
- **Provide education** and resources for new operators
- **Foster fellowship** among radio enthusiasts
- **Support emergency communications** readiness
- **Preserve the traditions** of amateur radio while embracing new technologies

## Club History

More Radio was founded by a group of passionate operators who wanted to create an inclusive and active ham radio community. Since our founding, we've grown to include members with diverse interests spanning HF DXing, VHF/UHF operations, digital modes, satellite communications, and more.

## Getting Involved

There are many ways to participate in our club:

- **Attend meetings** - Join us at our regular club meetings
- **Check into nets** - Participate in our weekly net sessions
- **Join a project** - Help with antenna parties, field day, and special events
- **Mentor new hams** - Share your knowledge with newcomers

## Licensing

New to amateur radio? Getting licensed is easier than you might think! The FCC issues three license classes:

1. **Technician** - Entry level license with VHF/UHF privileges
2. **General** - Expanded HF privileges for worldwide communication
3. **Amateur Extra** - Full privileges on all amateur bands

We can help you find testing sessions and study resources. Contact us to learn more!
