## Club Meetings

We hold regular meetings to discuss club business, share knowledge, and enjoy fellowship with other amateur radio operators. All licensed operators and those interested in amateur radio are welcome to attend.

### Regular Meetings

**When:** Second Thursday of each month
**Time:** 7:00 PM local time
**Where:** Community Center, Meeting Room B

### Meeting Format

Our typical meeting agenda includes:

1. **Welcome & Introductions** - Meet new members and visitors
2. **Club Business** - Treasury report, announcements, and committee updates
3. **Technical Presentation** - Educational talks on various ham radio topics
4. **Show & Tell** - Members share projects, new equipment, or interesting contacts
5. **Open Discussion** - Q&A, elmering, and general conversation
6. **Adjournment** - Informal socializing continues for those who wish to stay

### Special Events

Throughout the year, we host special events including:

- **Field Day** (June) - Our biggest operating event
- **Winter Holiday Party** (December) - Annual celebration
- **Hamfest** - Periodic swap meets and equipment sales
- **VE Testing Sessions** - License exam opportunities

### Can't Attend in Person?

We understand that not everyone can make it to in-person meetings. Check with club officers about remote participation options.
